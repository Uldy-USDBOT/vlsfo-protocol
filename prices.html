VLSFO and MGO Price Analysis

Understanding the price spread between Very Low Sulphur Fuel Oil (VLSFO) and Marine Gas Oil (MGO) is crucial for shipping efficiency. While VLSFO remains the primary choice for global fleets, MGO is essential for operations in Emission Control Areas (ECA).
