What is VLSFO? Meaning & Density Explained

VLSFO meaning refers to Very Low Sulfur Fuel Oil (max 0.50% sulfur). Fleet operators must carefully monitor VLSFO density (920 - 970 kg/m³) and viscosity. Incorrect density calculations can lead to separator issues and potential engine failure during ocean transit.

Is VLSFO Diesel? Understanding the Blend

"Is VLSFO diesel?" No. While Marine Gas Oil (MGO) is similar to diesel, VLSFO is a blended residual oil. It combines heavy residues with low-sulfur distillates to meet environmental regulations while keeping costs lower than pure marine diesel.

ISO 8217 Standards & Cat Fines

Quality matters as much as price. High levels of catalytic fines (cat fines) in VLSFO can destroy ship engines. We strongly advocate for rigorous testing and strict adherence to the latest ISO 8217 specifications to ensure safe and reliable voyage operations.
