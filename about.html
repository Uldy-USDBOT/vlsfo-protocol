About VLSFO.GLOBAL

VLSFO.GLOBAL is an independent market intelligence platform.

We provide daily Very Low Sulphur Fuel Oil (VLSFO) bunker prices, IMO 2020 compliance data, and technical analysis on blockchain applications in the maritime fuel sector for key ports including Singapore, Rotterdam, and Fujairah.

Important Notice: VLSFO.GLOBAL is not owned by, affiliated with, or endorsed by Integr8 Fuels, Ship & Bunker, Bunker Index, or any fuel supplier or trading company. We are a neutral, independent information source.

Our Mission

To provide transparent, real-time VLSFO pricing and connect traditional bunker markets with new technologies like blockchain settlement and AI-driven market analysis.

Data Sources

Our price data is aggregated from public market sources and updated daily. For official contract pricing, please refer to Price Reporting Agencies such as S&P Global Platts or Argus Media.
